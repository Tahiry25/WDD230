ABOUT US

Bountiful Foods

We initially made drinks to support our local soccer team players.

Hatso [huh-tsoo]'s son was a member of the local soccer team in Carlsbad back in 1993. She never missed a game when her son was playing. She would always bring drinks to each game to support her son. As time went by, these simple, yet special drinks that she made became famous among the players and soon... were requested by the whole stadium. And that is when Bountiful Foods was born. Hatsu's drinks were loved by many and even today, they are constantly being requested by the locals and tourists. Thank you Hatsu!
