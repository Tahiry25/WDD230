White Space and Clean Design

Apple Inc.

www.apple.com

This is Apple Inc.'s website. I believe that not only does whitespace give their website a simple, clean and minimalist look and feel that is so desirable in to look at but it really allows content to be more digestible and easy to understand to the user. Apple uses whitespace to draw the users attention to specific elements on the page but also they use them to guide the user through the content. And it works.

Proximity

Rebook Inc.

www.rebook.com

Besides whitespace, grouping related items will help users scan and understand your web pages quickly and easily. Logically grouping elements will help users to easily glance at it to understand the information.

Repetition

Amazon Inc.

www.amazon.com

Amazon uses repetition throughout their website in order to introduce new products or features yet familiar to the users. I believe as humans, we intuitively look for patterns. We look for something that is familiar in order to be comfortable. And I think this is where repetition becomes important in design to help user find what they are looking with ease and more quickly.
