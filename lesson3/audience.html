Target Audience

The target audience for this website are primarily the people within the city area as well as businesses in and near Saratoga.

Persona 1: Local

James just recently graduated and received his Master's degree in statistics. He uses the Chamber of Commerce to look for all the different businesses in town that might be a good fit for his education and experiences.

Persona 2: Potential Business Owner

After he discovers the multiple businesses in the city, James decided to create his own analysis and consultant company. He uses the chamber of commerce to find any existing companies that may be in the same field as his company and learn more about their business models and may be even learn from them.

Scenarios

  • How will the chamber of commerce help locals find nearby businesses.
  • The Chamber will be used as a resource and provide the people in the community multiple job openings.
  • The website help a potential future business owner to understand the business model of an existing company.
  • The website creates relationships between businesses that may lead to partnership.
